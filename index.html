Martin's Tips To Happy Fish
  • Keep water between 60 and 90 degrees fahrenheit. This ensures all fish are within their optimal water temperatures.
  • Change the water temperature frequently. This allows all fish to feel comfortable at some point.
  • Windex the inside and outside of the tank frequently. This allows you to see the fish better (and they can see you better, too).
  • Throw theme parties. Fish love a good party.
  • Change the arrangement of your tank weekly. This simulates the ever-changing ocean the fish are accustomed to.
